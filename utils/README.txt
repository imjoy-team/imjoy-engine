This folder contains files for running the ImJoy plugin engine used with https://ImJoy.io

For MacOS: copy ImJoyEngine.app to your Applications folder, right click and select open for the first time, later just click to launch it.

For Windows: copy ImJoyEngine.exe together with ImJoyEngine.exe.config to a folder (e.g. C:\\ImJoy), you can directly double click to launch the Plugin Engine. Optionally, you can right click on ImJoyEngine.exe send a shortcut to your desktop.

For Linux: copy ImJoyEngine.sh to the folder you want to store it(e.g. your home folder), and launch it with `bash ImJoyEngine.sh` (sh ImJoyEngine.sh does not work in our test).
